Tutorial

How to make any song slowed and reverb

❌ The Old Way

Historically, making a slowed and reverb track required downloading the MP3, opening expensive audio software like FL Studio or Audacity, manually stretching the tempo, adding a reverb plugin, and exporting the file. It took 15 minutes just to hear a single song differently.

✅ The BetterCallSlow Way

Now, you can make any song slow and reverb directly in your browser. Just install the BetterCallSlow extension, play any track on YouTube, Spotify, or SoundCloud, and drag the speed and reverb sliders. The audio is processed in real-time, completely free, with no downloads required.
